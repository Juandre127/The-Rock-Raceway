The Rock Raceway

Home of Motorsports

Situated in Brakpan - Gauteng, THE ROCK RACEWAY is a Motorsport Facility that hosts Oval Racing, Drifting and Drag Racing. Headed by Tim Stephens AKA 'Mr Rock' and flying under the WOMZA flagship, you are bound to be entertained!

What is better than witnessing a bunch of Adrenalin Junkies putting on a fantastic show for you, the smell of race fuel and the sound of modified engines revving high with smoking tyres ripping up some tar on any given night! This is the place to be if you enjoy all of the above!
